CU Girls Hostel

University experience is an important milestone in an individual’s life. This time period will always be remembered in your life bundled with many exciting and sweet memories.

Living in a hostel requires a high degree of integrity, consciousness, cooperation, tolerance and discipline. Students who successfully complete their stay in the hostel become independent and responsible human beings.

Hostels at Chandigarh University can rightly be called a “home away from home”. Our hostels provide the most conducive environment for students’ holistic development. The rooms are well-furnished and equipped with modern amenities and hygienic conditions.

Chandigarh University has named its Girl’s Hostels on the name of some iconic personalities & places:

Sukhna Hostel

A peaceful hostel inspired by the charm of Sukhna Lake.

Tagore Hostel

Named after Rabindranath Tagore, promoting intellect and creativity.

Gobind Hostel

A warm, family-like hostel offering comfort and strong values.

Shivalik Hostel

Named after Shivalik Hills, symbolizing high aspirations and growth.

Le Corbusier Hostel

Named after the renowned architect “Le Corbusier.” Just as he redefined urban spaces, this hostel inspires girls to break boundaries, cultivate discipline, and shape their future with a strong foundation.

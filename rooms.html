ROOM ALLOTMENT

  • The hostel rooms are allocated online.
  • Student will receive the message of room allotment through SMS/Email.
  • Once the room is allotted, it can be changed only under exceptional circumstances with the due approval of the competent authority (Hostel).
  • Rooms are allotted as per availability and first come first serve basis. Foreign/NRI students may reserve their rooms in advance.
  • Every attempt will be made to provide hostel accommodation to all the desirable students.
  • The room allotted to the student will be cancelled if s/he fails to occupy the room within the stipulated time.
  • Once a student vacates the hostel voluntarily or is required to leave for any reason, s/he will not be eligible for reallocation of hostel accommodation.
Re-Allotment

Room allotment is for one year, and the re-allotment process is conducted online through CUIMS at the beginning of each academic year. Rooms are assigned based on seniority, with the senior-most batches allocated to the second floor, while junior batches are accommodated on higher levels.

Furniture

Each room can accommodate four students and is furnished with essential amenities provided by the University, including beds, built-in study tables, chairs, almirahs, desert coolers, and ducting (AC) for all facilities. Students may bring small personal items such as a side table or dustbin; however, bulky or oversized items are not permitted.

Note: At the time of possession, the student needs to ensure that all above-mentioned items are present in the room. If not, the student should report immediately to the hostel warden.

At the time of vacating the hostel, the student needs to hand over all the above-mentioned items in serviceable condition.

Electricity

Regular supply of electricity is provided to the students in all weathers. Our hostels have 24x7 DG power backup in case of power failure.

Students should keep their rooms neat and clean at all times. They shall be jointly or individually responsible for the furniture and fixtures present in their rooms at the time of the allotment of the room. If any student finds anything damaged or defective in the furniture or fixture in their allotted room or finds anything missing upon occupying the room, it will be her duty to bring it to the notice of the Warden. If the student does not point out any deficiency, it will be presumed that everything is in order.

Student Activities & Committes

Vibrant campus life with events, celebrations & student committee

Student Activities

Hostel Night:

The new students are formally welcomed with a fresher’s party. Various events like dance, songs, and plays are organized during the party. One outstanding student from the fresher’s is selected as Ms. Fresher.

Other Activities:

Various other activities like DJ Party, Debate Competition and Expert Talks etc. are organized to inculcate necessary life skills in the students.

Festival Celebrations:

University is significantly known for its unity in diversity. Keeping this in view, the boarders fill the hostels with colors of integrity in each festival.

Note: No celebration without prior permission of the concerned authority is allowed in the hostel premises.

Committees

Mess Committee:

The Mess Committee of the Hostel is formed to incorporate the views and ideas of the students related to the menu served. It acts as a communication channel for student concerns and menu suggestions. Comprises:

  • DSW – In charge
  • Assistant Director – Member
  • Manager – Member
  • Senior Warden – Member
  • Wardens – Member
  • Students – Member
Health & Hygiene Committee:

Formed to ensure hygienic conditions in the hostel and mess.

  • Assistant Director – In charge
  • Manager – Member
  • Senior Warden – Member
  • Wardens – Member
  • Students – Member
Hostel Committee:

Formed at hostel level to oversee maintenance, handle complaints, and ensure smooth functioning of amenities and cleanliness.

  • Manager – In charge
  • Senior Warden
  • Wardens
  • Students
Sports Committee:

Organizes various sports activities.

  • Assistant Director
  • Manager
  • Sports In-Charge
  • Wardens
  • Students
Cultural Committee:

Plans and organizes cultural events and ensures their smooth execution.

  • Assistant Director
  • Manager
  • Wardens
  • Students

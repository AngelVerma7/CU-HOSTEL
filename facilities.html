Mess Rules
  • All hostel students must have meals in the mess as long as they are on the rolls.
  • Strict discipline and proper decorum will be maintained in the dining hall.
  • Students are not allowed to visit the kitchen or store area.
  • No meals from other hostels are allowed.
  • Menu is designed by the mess committee and approved by DSW.
  • Seasonal availability of fruits/vegetables is ensured.
  • Menu changes only with DSW Office permission.
  • Menu is displayed on hostel notice boards.
  • Parents may dine as guests with prior Hostel Authority permission.
  • Food can be taken to the room only with warden’s permission if the student is ill.
  • Students must avoid food wastage.
  • Self-service system is followed in all messes.
  • >Biometric authentication is mandatory to receive meals
  • Students must follow designated mess timings.
  • Misbehavior with mess staff will be treated as indiscipline.
  • Providing quality food is a privilege, not a right.
  • No utensils or food are allowed in hostel rooms.

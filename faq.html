Hostel Gate Timings:
• Weekdays: 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM
• Saturday & Sunday: 5:30 AM - 8:00 PM

Late entry requires prior permission from the warden and is limited to 2 times per month.

Essential Items to Bring:
• Personal clothing and toiletries
• Bed sheets, pillows, and blankets
• Study materials and stationery
• Personal laptop/computer
• Medical prescriptions and first aid
• Identity documents and photographs

Items Provided: Bed, mattress, study table, chair, wardrobe, and basic furniture.

Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi (100+ Mbps) is available throughout the hostel including all rooms. Each room also has ethernet ports for wired connections. The internet service is available 24/7 at no additional cost.

Security Features:
• 24/7 CCTV surveillance
• Biometric entry system
• Round-the-clock security guards
• Visitor management system
• Emergency communication systems
• Restricted access to residential floors
• Regular security patrols

Room changes are possible but require a valid reason and written application to the warden. Common reasons include medical issues, compatibility problems with roommates, or academic requirements. Administrative charges may apply for voluntary changes, and you must vacate the current room within 48 hours of approval.

Application Process:
1. Submit online application through university portal
2. Pay application fee and security deposit
3. Submit required documents (ID proof, medical certificate, passport photos)
4. Attend orientation if required
5. Receive room allocation confirmation
6. Complete check-in formalities

Timeline: Apply at least 30 days before intended move-in date.

Required Documents:
• University admission letter
• Identity proof (Aadhar Card/Passport)
• Address proof
• Medical fitness certificate
• Passport size photographs (6 copies)
• Parent/Guardian consent letter
• Academic transcripts
• Hostel application form (available on portal)
• Income certificate (for fee concessions)

Yes, during peak admission periods, there may be a waiting list. Priority is given based on:
• Distance from home location
• Academic merit
• Application date and time
• Special circumstances (medical, financial)

Students on the waiting list are notified as soon as rooms become available.

Yes, you can request a specific roommate during the application process. Both students must mention each other's names in their applications. However, final allocation depends on availability and administrative approval. Roommate requests are accommodated on a first-come, first-served basis.

Mess Timings:
• Breakfast: 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM(Weekdays) & 8:00 AM -9:30 AM(Weekends)
• Lunch: 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM
• Evening Snacks: 4:30 PM - 5:15 PM
• Dinner: 7:30 PM - 9:00 PM

Menu: Balanced vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals with regional and North Indian cuisine. Special dietary requirements can be accommodated with prior notice.

Yes, comprehensive laundry services are available:
• Self-service washing machines and dryers
• Iron and ironing boards on each floor
• Professional laundry service (charges apply)
• Laundry soap and detergent vending machines

Self-service is free, while professional service costs ₹300 per month.

Medical Support:
• On-campus medical center with qualified staff
• 24/7 first aid availability
• Emergency medical assistance
• Tie-up with nearby hospitals
• Medical insurance guidance
• Mental health counseling services

A qualified medical assistant is available round the clock for emergencies.

Absolutely! All hostel residents have free access to:
• Fully equipped gymnasium
• Yoga and meditation room
• Indoor games room (table tennis, carrom, chess)
• Library and study areas
• TV lounge and recreation room
• Terrace garden

Some facilities have specific timings, which are posted on the notice boards.

Timings for the GYM are: Gym Timings:
Morning:
• 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM (1st batch)
• 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM (2nd batch)
Evening:
• 4:30 PM to 5:30 PM (1st batch)
• 5:30 PM to 6:30 PM (2nd batch)
• 6:30 PM to 7:30 PM (3rd batch)

Drinking Water: Water coolers are provided in the hostels on every floor to meet the drinking water needs of the students. The hostels are equipped with water purifying systems that supply clean and safe water.

Hot Water (Geyser): Available during winters at the following scheduled timings:
• Morning Timing: 6:00 AM to 9:00 AM
• Evening Timing: 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM

Saturday, Sunday & Holiday Timing:
• Morning: 7:00 AM to 9:30 AM
• Evening: 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM

Prohibited Items:
• Alcohol and tobacco products
• Illegal drugs or substances
• Weapons or sharp objects
• Cooking appliances (except permitted ones like electric kettle)
• Pets or animals
• Inflammable materials
• High-power electrical appliances

Violation of these rules may result in disciplinary action.

Visitor Guidelines:
• Weekdays: 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM
• Weekends: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
• All visitors must register at reception
• Valid ID proof mandatory
• Maximum 2 visitors per resident
• Visitors restricted to lobby and visitor rooms
• No visitors during exam periods
• Overnight stay requires special permission

Disciplinary Actions:
• First Warning: Verbal warning for minor violations
• Written Warning: Formal written warning
• Fine: Monetary penalty for repeated violations
• Suspension: Temporary suspension for serious misconduct
• Rustication: Permanent expulsion for major violations

Students have the right to appeal disciplinary decisions through the hostel committee.

Study hours are from 8:00 PM to 11:00 PM, during which silence is mandatory in residential areas. You can leave the hostel during this time, but you must inform the floor supervisor and return before the hostel closing time. Academic activities and library visits are encouraged during these hours.

Included in Hostel Fees:
• Accommodation charges
• Mess fees (4 meals per day)
• Electricity and water charges
• Wi-Fi internet access
• Basic furniture and fixtures
• Security services
• Maintenance and housekeeping
• Access to all common facilities

Additional Charges: Laundry service (₹300/month),damage charges (as applicable)

Payment Schedule:
• Semester 1: 60% of annual fee (due by July 31st)
• Semester 2: 40% of annual fee (due by December 31st)
• Security Deposit: At the time of admission
• Late Fee: ₹500 charged after due date

Payment reminders are sent via email and SMS before due dates.

Refund Policy:
• Security Deposit: Fully refundable (minus any damage charges)
• Before Semester Start: 90% refund of fees paid
• Within 1 Month: 50% refund of fees paid
• After 1 Month: No refund of accommodation fees
• Mess Fees: Prorated refund for unused portion

Refund processing takes 30-45 working days after approval.

Available Concessions:
• Merit-based scholarships (up to 100% fee waiver)
• Need-based financial assistance
• SC/ST category concessions as per government norms
• Economically weaker section (EWS) benefits
>
Students must provide relevant documentation and apply through the scholarship CUCET

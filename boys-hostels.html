CU Boys Hostel

University experience is an important milestone in an individual’s life. This time period will always be remembered in your life, bundled with many exciting and sweet memories.

Living in a hostel requires a high degree of exciting and consciousness, cooperation, tolerance and discipline. Students, who successfully complete their stay in the hostel, become independent and responsible human beings.

Hostels at Chandigarh University can be rightly called a “home away from home”. Our hostels provide the most conducive environment for student’s holistic development. The hostels have well furnished rooms equipped with the necessary facilities and amenities. The University hostel has aesthetic surrounding and hygienic conditions.

Chandigarh University has named its hostels on the name of some well know ICONIC Personalities and places of India :-

Nek Chand Hostel

It is named after the much acclaimed, Rock Garden designer. The hostel comprises Six Towers and reminds the inmates of the beauty and artistic creativity of Nek Chand.

Zakir Hostel

Named after India’s 3rd President, Zakir Hussain, this hostel inspires knowledge, wisdom, and refinement.

City Hostel

City Hostel 1 TDI and City Hostel 3 Toronto offer comforts and facilities that make students feel at home.

North Campus Hostels

Consisting of NCH-A and NCH-B blocks, these hostels offer a modern and disciplined atmosphere for residents.

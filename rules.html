Rules & Regulations

Guidelines for a safe and harmonious living environment

Entry & Exit Timings

Hostel Gate Timings
  • Weekdays: 5:30 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Saturday: 5:30 AM - 11:00 PM
  • Sunday & Holidays: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Emergency entry available with prior permission
Late Entry Policy
  • Late entry requires prior permission from warden
  • Maximum 2 late entries per month allowed
  • Valid reason must be provided
  • Parent/Guardian consent required for repeated late entries
Leave & Outing Rules
  • Outstation leave requires written application
  • Local outings must be informed to floor supervisor
  • Weekend leave requires parent consent
  • Maximum 4 days leave per month for non-academic purposes

Code of Conduct

General Behavior
  • Maintain respectful behavior with staff and residents
  • Use polite language in common areas
  • Respect privacy of other residents
  • Follow queue discipline in mess and common facilities
Room Discipline
  • Keep rooms clean and organized
  • No loud music or noise after 10:00 PM
  • Room sharing only with prior approval
  • Report maintenance issues immediately
Prohibited Items
  • Alcohol and tobacco products
  • Illegal drugs or substances
  • Weapons or sharp objects
  • Cooking appliances (except permitted ones)
  • Pets or animals
Academic Discipline
  • Maintain minimum 75% attendance
  • Study hours: 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM (silence mandatory)
  • Library and study room usage as per schedule
  • Academic misconduct will result in hostel action

Visitor Guidelines

Visitor Timings
  • Weekdays: 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Weekends: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • No visitors allowed during exam periods
  • Prior permission required for extended visits
Visitor Registration
  • All visitors must register at reception
  • Valid ID proof mandatory
  • Visitor pass to be displayed at all times
  • Resident responsible for visitor's conduct
Permitted Visitors
  • Parents and immediate family members
  • Female friends and relatives
  • Male visitors only in designated areas with permission
  • University faculty and staff
Visitor Guidelines
  • Visitors not allowed in residential floors
  • Meeting areas limited to lobby and visitor rooms
  • Maximum 2 visitors per resident at a time
  • Overnight stay requires special permission

Safety & Security

Personal Safety
  • Always carry hostel ID card
  • Do not share room keys or access cards
  • Lock rooms when leaving
  • Report suspicious activities immediately
Fire Safety
  • Familiarize yourself with emergency exits
  • Do not tamper with fire safety equipment
  • No candles or open flames in rooms
  • Participate in fire drill exercises
Health & Hygiene
  • Maintain personal hygiene standards
  • Report illness to warden immediately
  • Use designated areas for laundry
  • Dispose of waste in designated bins
Emergency Procedures
  • Emergency contact numbers displayed prominently
  • First aid kit available on each floor
  • Medical emergencies: Contact warden immediately
  • Fire/Natural disasters: Follow evacuation plan

Miscellaneous Rules

Fees & Payments
  • Hostel fees to be paid by due date
  • Late payment charges applicable
  • No refund for early vacation
  • Damage charges deducted from security deposit
Room Allocation & Changes
  • Room change only with valid reason
  • Written application required for room change
  • Administrative charges for voluntary changes
  • Vacate room within 48 hours of notice
Disciplinary Actions
  • Warning for first-time minor violations
  • Fine for repeated violations
  • Suspension for serious misconduct
  • Permanent rustication for major violations
Complaint & Grievance
  • Submit complaints in writing to warden
  • Hostel committee meetings monthly
  • Anonymous suggestion box available
  • Escalation process for unresolved issues
Important Notice

All residents are expected to read and follow these rules. Violation of rules may result in disciplinary action including suspension or termination of hostel accommodation.

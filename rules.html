Rules & Regulations

Guidelines for a safe and harmonious living environment

General Hostel Rules

  1. Hostel session starts from 1 July to 30 June every year.
  2. Every resident is required to mark their biometric attendance between 8:00 pm and 9:00 pm each night...
  3. The student will return to the Hostel before 7:30 pm in summers and 6:30 pm in winters after which gates will be closed. Latecomers will have to make an entry in the register kept at the University main gate, which will be informed to their hostel warden, their parents and local guardians.
  4. The student is permitted to go out of station to participate in various competitions only after submitting proof and application and permission from HOD/Parents. The student will intimate the warden and make an entry in the night out register kept with the warden.
  5. The student shall take prior permission from the Sr. Warden to celebrate any festival and birthday. The birthday celebration should be done at a common place for one to two hours preferably between 6:00 pm to 8:00 pm and that too in the mess.
  6. The student will always carry their identity cards provided to them by the university when leaving the campus, so that the university can be contacted and informed in case of an emergency.
  7. Visitors and parents are permitted to meet the student only in the designated visitor’s lobby, located on the ground floor of each hostel.
  8. The students shall keep their rooms, balcony and surrounding areas tidy, neat and clean at all times and shall not litter in the hostel premises.
  9. No wet clothes should be dried on the furniture or in corridors.
  10. The warden may confiscate any such clothes hung for drying in the corridors.
  11. Students are advised to keep their valuables under lock and key.
  12. Students are responsible for their belongings. Any negligence on their part will be their sole responsibility.
  13. Students in each room are jointly and independently responsible for the furniture, fittings, etc. Any damage to the hostel property will be recovered from them.
  14. In case an individual is not identified with respect to the damage done to the property, a collective fine on all concerned students can be imposed to recover the cost of damage to the property.
  15. Students will be expelled from the hostel for repeated violation of rules and misconduct such as theft, ragging, abnormal behavior, use of narcotic substances, alcohol, and indiscipline etc.
  16. If you are assigned a 4-seater room and two occupants move out, the remaining two residents will be re-assigned to a different room.
  17. No student should be present in their room during university hours. If the student is unwell, prior permission should be taken to use the room.
  18. No student is allowed to keep any type of medicine in her possession without it being prescribed by the medical officer of the campus. Students receiving treatment outside the campus should provide medical history to the warden and the medical officer. In the case of prescribed medicines like antidepressants or anticonvulsants will be given by the medical team.
  19. Students have to inform their respective wardens regarding any change in their parental/native address and contact number.
  20. No student is allowed to invite any day scholar to the hostel premises. Students who do not fulfill eligibility of attendance as per norms can be asked to vacate the hostel without any refund.
  21. No student will organize any kind of meeting on the premises to unite or instigate students for any kind of violent activity against the university.
  22. CU authorities reserve the right to carry out surprise checks at any desired time.
  23. No student should disregard her seniors and act aggressively towards her juniors. The noticeboard is provided on the hostel premises to appraise the students about any information sent by the authorities.
  24. Suggestions/Complaints related to food and other hostel services should be reported to the hostel warden.
  25. No student is allowed to take utensils from the mess to her room. If any utensils are found in the room, then an appropriate fine will be charged. Pets are strictly prohibited in the hostel.
  26. No student is allowed to keep her personal conveyance on the hostel premises.
  27. During the Internship/training period comprising of a full semester, the students will be exempted from paying the mess charges.
  28. Students should not change/exchange the rooms without the prior written permission from the Assistant/Associate Director of hostels.
  29. All the rooms, walls, doors & windows should be kept clean and tidy. No posters or photographs should be pasted on the room walls/doors/cupboards.
  30. Residents must switch off fans and lights whenever they go out of their rooms to conserve electricity. Defaulters can be penalized.
  31. Residents should not issue orders to hostel employees or interfere in their functioning. Misconduct by hostel employees, if any, should be reported to the Dean Student Welfare.
  32. All hostel residents are expected to be well-versed in the rules and regulations. Ignorance of these rules will not be considered a valid excuse under any circumstances.
  33. Students vacating the hostel must remove all their belongings within 24 hours after the conclusion of the semester examinations.
  34. If any student leaves the university’s hostel premises without the permission of the respective authorities, Chandigarh University will not be responsible for any mishap/inappropriate behavior that occurs outside the campus.
  35. Students must refrain from engaging in inappropriate activities, including physical assault and property damage.
  36. Bathroom slippers are not allowed in the mess.
  37. Room lights do not to be switched on after 11:00 pm.
  38. Friends from other rooms are not allowed without permission.
  39. Students cannot take a day-out or night-out during attendance time (8:00 PM to 9:00 PM). They can take it after 9:00 PM.
  40. If you are assigned a 4-seater room and two occupants move out, the remaining two residents will be re-assigned to a different room.

Leave Rules

  1. A hosteller can avail a day leave from 8:00 am to 7:30 pm (Holiday) during summers, 8:00 am to 6:30 pm during winter.
  2. Day leave during the five working days can be taken with due permission of the concerned HOD.
  3. During weekends/holidays, permission from the warden is sufficient.
  4. Night out leave is given to the hostellers when they wish to go home to see their parents or local guardian after permission from parents.
  5. Student can leave the campus only with the prior permission taken from their warden in writing. Students have to show the same permission at the gate before leaving.
  6. Students should get their leave booklet signed by their warden at least one night before. On return, it is mandatory to get it signed by their parents and at the university gate.
  7. No residents are permitted to leave the hostel after 6:00 pm. In case of emergency, parents or local guardians may pick them up with prior permission of the Senior Warden.

Note: If a student does not report back after the allotted period of leave, her room allotment will be canceled.

Study Room Rules

  • Using mobile phones inside the study room is strictly prohibited.
  • Students are not supposed to create any nuisance for others in the study room.
  • Before coming or leaving the study room, making an entry in the register is mandatory.
  • Students should switch off the fans and tube lights before leaving the study room.

Hostel Leaving Procedure

If a student does not want to continue the hostel facility at the end of the academic session, she must fill a discontinuation form and submit it to the respective warden after parents’ consent.

Checklist: Documents to Attach (Photocopy)
  • Photocopy of University & Hostel Fee Receipt issued by Accounts Office.
  • 4 passport-size photographs of the student, parents, and 2 copies each of visitors and local guardian.
  • Photocopy of marriage certificate (for married students).
  • Photocopy of ID proof of parents and local guardian.
  • Medical documents, in case of health issues (if applicable).

Note: Candidates must be accompanied by their Parents/Local Guardians at the time of admission. For a local guardian, an authentic authorization from the parents is required. In no case shall the local guardian be a student.

Visitor Guidelines

Visitor Timings
  • Weekdays: 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM
  • Weekends: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM
  • No visitors allowed during exam periods
  • Prior permission required for extended visits
Visitor Registration
  • All visitors must register at reception
  • Valid ID proof mandatory
  • Visitor pass to be displayed at all times
  • Resident responsible for visitor’s conduct
Permitted Visitors
  • Parents and immediate family members
  • Female friends and relatives
  • Male visitors only in designated areas with permission
  • University faculty and staff
Visitor Guidelines
  • Visitors not allowed in residential floors
  • Meeting areas limited to lobby and visitor rooms
  • Maximum 2 visitors per resident at a time
  • Overnight stay requires special permission

Safety & Security

Personal Safety
  • Always carry hostel ID card
  • Do not share room keys or access cards
  • Lock rooms when leaving
  • Report suspicious activities immediately
Fire Safety
  • Familiarize yourself with emergency exits
  • Do not tamper with fire safety equipment
  • No candles or open flames in rooms
  • Participate in fire drill exercises
Health & Hygiene
  • Maintain personal hygiene standards
  • Report illness to warden immediately
  • Use designated areas for laundry
  • Dispose of waste in designated bins
Emergency Procedures
  • Emergency contact numbers displayed prominently
  • First aid kit available on each floor
  • Medical emergencies: Contact warden immediately
  • Fire/Natural disasters: Follow evacuation plan

Miscellaneous Rules

Fees & Payments
  • Hostel fees to be paid by due date
  • Late payment charges applicable
  • No refund for early vacation
  • Damage charges deducted from security deposit
Room Allocation & Changes
  • Room change only with valid reason
  • Written application required for room change
  • Administrative charges for voluntary changes
  • Vacate room within 48 hours of notice
Disciplinary Actions
  • Warning for first-time minor violations
  • Fine for repeated violations
  • Suspension for serious misconduct
  • Permanent rustication for major violations
Complaint & Grievance
  • Submit complaints in writing to warden
  • Hostel committee meetings monthly
  • Anonymous suggestion box available
  • Escalation process for unresolved issues

List of Items Required at the Time of Joining

Bedding: One mattress, two bed sheets, one pillow and two pillow covers, one quilt, one blanket. Bed sheets should be for a standard size single bed in university hostel rooms. (Mattress and pillow will also be available in tuck shops for purchase).

  • One lock with five keys for the room
  • One small lock for the almirah
  • One bucket
  • One mug
  • One dustbin
  • One doormat
  • One small sized wiper and personal toiletry
  • One plate
  • One bowl
  • One glass
  • One water bottle
  • One spoon

Prohibited Items

  • Knives, weapons of any type
  • Alcohol (Liquor)
  • Drugs
  • Medicines (unless prescription is provided)
  • Personal vehicles
  • Electronic iron, induction, kettle, immersion heater rod, electronic cooker, music system or any other electronic items
Important Notice

All residents are expected to read and follow these rules. Violation of rules may result in disciplinary action including suspension or termination of hostel accommodation.
